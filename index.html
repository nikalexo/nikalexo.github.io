As of February 2024, I am an MSCA postdoctoral fellow at Prof. Spinellis' Business Analytics Laboratory of the Athens University of Economics and Business. My MSCA project is SPUCS, aiming to provide users more control over their smart devices via transparency mechanisms and novel software architectures. Before joining AUEB, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Telecooperation Lab of TU Darmstadt, Germany, where I had previously become a Dr.-Ing. with my dissertation titled New Approaches to Software Security Metrics and Measurements. I am interested in all aspects of computer security and privacy, with a focus on metrics and measurements of software security, transparency for mobile devices, and decentralized computing. For more information about me you can take a look at my CV.
